A reading list for the long work.

Books and essays we return to again and again — to understand how structural racism was built, and how it can be undone.

Historical Narratives & Origins of Race

📖 A People’s History of the United States — Howard Zinn

📖 Anti-Racism in US History: The First 200 Years — Herbert Aptheker

📖 Before the Mayflower: A History of Black America, 1619-1962 — LeRone Bennett

📖 Europe and the People Without History — Eric R. Wolf

📖 The White Man’s Burden: Historical Origins of Racism in America — Winthrop D. Jordan

Critical Race Theory & Analysis

📖 Critical Race Theory: An Introduction — Richard Delgado & Jean Stefancic

📖 Faces at the Bottom of the Well: The Permanence of Racism — Derek Bell

📖 Race Matters in Child Welfare — Dennette Derezotes et al.

📖 The Souls of Black Folk — W.E.B. DuBois

Systemic Analysis & Social Policy

📖 Black Wealth/White Wealth: A New Perspective on Racial Inequality — Oliver & Shapiro

📖 Racist America: Roots, Current Realities, and Future Reparations — Joe R. Feagin

📖 Unequal Protection: Environmental Justice and Communities of Color — Robert Bullard (Ed.)

📖 The Color of Wealth: The Story Behind the U.S. Racial Wealth Divide — Meizhu Lui et al.

Law, Criminal Justice, and Incarceration

📖 Race-Based Judgments, Race-Neutral Justifications — Samuel Sommers & Michael Norton

📖 Reducing Racial Disparity in the Criminal Justice System — The Sentencing Project

📖 Slavery By Another Name (Book & Documentary) — Douglas Blackmon

📖 The New Jim Crow: Mass Incarceration in the Age of Colorblindness — Michelle Alexander

📖 White By Law: The Legal Construction of Race — Ian Haney Lopez

Education and Pedagogy

📖 Are Teachers’ Expectations Different For Racial Minority Than For European American Students? — Journal of Educational Psychology

📖 Beyond Heroes and Holidays — Enid Lee et al.

📖 Lies My Teacher Told Me: Everything Your American History Textbook Got Wrong — James Loewen

📖 Pedagogy of the Oppressed — Paulo Freire

📖 White Awareness: Handbook for Anti-racism Training — Judith Katz

Health and Social Outcomes

📖 Being Black, Living in the Red: Race, Wealth and Social Policy in America — Dalton Conley

📖 Multiple Pathways Linking Racism to Health Outcomes — CJ Harrell et al.

📖 Reducing Racial and Social-Class Inequalities in Health — S. Leonard Syme

📖 Unnatural Causes: Is Inequality Making Us Sick? (Documentary) — California Newsreel

Implicit Bias, Discrimination, and Stereotypes

📖 Are Emily and Greg More Employable Than Lakisha and Jamal? — Marianne Bertrand & Sendhil Mullainathan

📖 Blind Spot: The Hidden Biases of Good People — Mahzarin Banaji & Anthony Greenwald

📖 Implicit Bias: Scientific Foundations — A.G. Greenwald & L. H. Krieger

📖 Whistling Vivaldi: How Stereotypes Affect Us and What We Can Do — Claude Steele

Economic Inequality and Labor Discrimination

📖 Forty Million Dollar Slaves — William C. Rhoden

📖 The Hidden Cost of Being African-American — Thomas Shapiro

📖 The Color of Discipline: Sources of Racial and Gender Disproportionality in School Punishment — R. Skiba et al.

Identity, Race, and Cultural Representation

📖 Black and White Styles in Conflict — Thomas Kochman

📖 How Jews Became White Folks and What That Says About Race in America — Karen Brodkin

📖 How the Irish Became White — Noel Ignatiev

📖 The Possessive Investment in Whiteness — George Lipsitz

📖 Working Toward Whiteness: How America’s Immigrants Became White — David Roediger

Native American and Indigenous Studies

📖 Bury My Heart at Wounded Knee: An Indian History of the American West — Dee Brown

📖 The State of Native American Genocide, Colonization & Resistance — M. Annette Jaimes

📖 From a Native Daughter: Colonialism and Sovereignty in Hawaii — Haunani-Kay Trask

Intersectionality: Gender, Race, and Class

📖 Killing the Black Body: Race, Reproduction, and the Meaning of Liberty — Dorothy Roberts

📖 When and Where I Enter: The Impact of Black Women on Race and Sex in America — Paula Giddings

📖 Women, Race, and Class — Angela Davis

📖 Sisters of the Yam: Black Women and Self-Recovery — bell hooks

Activism and Social Justice

📖 Becoming an Anti-Racist Church — Joseph Barndt

📖 Reluctant Reformers: Racism and Social Reform Movements in the United States — Robert L. Allen

📖 Uprooting Racism: How White People Can Work for Racial Justice — Paul Kivel

📖 You Call This A Democracy: Who Benefits, Who Pays and Who Really Decides? — Paul Kivel

Reading is a start. Training goes deeper.
